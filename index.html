Who We Are

A Boutique Hospital
Built on Trust & Excellence

Franklyn Medical Centre was founded in 2007 by Dr. Lynda Decker Brewer with a clear vision: to deliver personalized, high-quality clinical care in a calm, professional, and dignified setting — redefining what private healthcare should feel like in Accra.

Unlike large, impersonal hospital networks, we operate as a boutique facility where every patient is known by name and every clinical decision is made with individual attention. Our signature Hub & Spoke model means you receive core GP care in-house, while our vetted network of top-tier specialists ensures seamless continuity when advanced consultation is required.

We serve individuals, families, and corporations alike — delivering the kind of proactive, relationship-based healthcare that prevents illness before it demands treatment.

Leadership

The Heart Behind Franklyn

Our centre is led by a visionary clinician who combined medical expertise with a genuine passion for redefining healthcare in Ghana.

Founder & Chief Executive

Dr. Lynda Decker Brewer

Founder & CEO

Dr. Lynda Decker Brewer is the architect of Franklyn Medical Centre's philosophy of care. Combining deep clinical knowledge with an acute understanding of what patients truly need, she established Franklyn in 2007 with a singular mission — to create a private healthcare environment in Accra that rivals international standards while remaining deeply rooted in the Ghanaian community.

Under her leadership, Franklyn has grown into one of Accra's most respected boutique hospitals, known not only for clinical excellence but for the warmth, discretion, and personal attention that defines every patient interaction. Dr. Brewer remains actively involved in clinical governance, corporate health partnerships, and the strategic direction of the centre.
